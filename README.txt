TITANIC DATASET
Aim
The goal of the analysis conducted is to determine the survival rates of various demographics aboard the Titanic.
Link to Tableau Public here: https://public.tableau.com/views/titanic_dashboard_17209553367570/Dashboard1?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link

The Titanic dataset is a popular one amongst beginners looking to delve into the world of data science. The essence of working on this project is to gain experience on analyzing data and sharpen my skills with various tools such as Excel and Tableau. I picked this data set because of it's simplicity and straightforwardness, the focus is directed towards the survival rates of people on the Titanic.

DATA SOURCE
The dataset was obtained from kaggle as it is a well known and reliable source for any kind of dataset.

RESULTS




From the images above, we can tell that more women survived the crash than men. Most of the survivors embarked from Southampton in comparison to other embarkment points. The oldest person to survive the crash was an 80 year old man and the youngest was a boy of 5 months. These are just some of the results obtained from the analysis conducted.

TECHNOLOGY 
Microsoft Excel and Tableau desktop were the tools used while conducting analysis.
